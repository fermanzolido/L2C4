    - Result * FuncMul (Buff) works.
3.  **Skill List**: Verified that `SkillList` packet iterates over player skills and sends the `passive` flag correctly.

Findings:
- The data flow from `Player` stats to `UserInfo` packet is correct.
- The `Calculator` correctly applies passive skills to stats.
- `SkillList` correctly populates the client's skill window.
- `UserInfo` packet structure appears consistent with L2J Mobius (High Five) standards, including the re-sending of P.Atk.Spd in the magic block.

Conclusion:
The "Status (Alt+T)" window data, including skills and passives, is verified to be handled correctly by the server code.
